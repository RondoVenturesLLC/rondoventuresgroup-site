Who We Are

A systems company built by someone who knows how agents actually work.

Rondo Ventures was founded by Ron Douglas — a project manager and systems builder who saw firsthand how real estate agents were being left behind by generic tech tools that didn't fit the way they actually worked.

We build intelligent, closed-loop systems that observe how an agent works — then automate it. Our products are practical, tested with real agents, and designed to scale without losing the personal touch that makes great agents great.

Every system we build follows one principle: define it, test it, lock it, and let it run. No loose ends. No drift. Just a repeatable process that gets better over time.
